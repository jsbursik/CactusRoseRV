What's nearby?

Stay on the easy side of I-20

Set in Mingus at 115 W I-20, our RV park gives you quick on/off access to the interstate with the quiet of a country stopover. It's a practical base for a single night or a long weekend—close to fuel, food, and the region's best outdoor spots.

Historic Thurber & local flavor

Just east, Thurber's 128-foot smokestack marks what was once a booming coal and brick town; today you can tour the story at the W.K. Gordon Center (open Tues-Sun). Classic small-town dining is minutes away too—Mary's Café in Strawn is a legendary Texas road-food stop.

Lakes, trails, and dinosaur tracks

Bring bikes or boots: Lake Mineral Wells State Park & Trailway offers miles of paths across the Cross Timbers, rock-climbing at Penitentiary Hollow, and lakeside picnics. Prefer big water? Possum Kingdom Lake and the state park around it are a favorite for boating and fishing. Dinosaur Valley State Park in Glen Rose makes an unforgettable family day trip with real dino tracks along the Paluxy River.

Coming soon: more to explore

Texas Parks & Wildlife is building Palo Pinto Mountains State Park not far from here—the first new state park in North Texas in 25 years—so expect even more hiking, fishing, and stargazing options as it opens.
